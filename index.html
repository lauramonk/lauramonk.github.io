Webpage of Laura Monk

I am a Lecturer at the University of Bristol (UK).

Before that, I did my PhD under the supervision of Nalini Anantharaman at the Université de Strasbourg, in France. I was also a guest at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn (Germany) in the team of Ursula Hamenstädt, as well as a Research Associate with Jens Marklof in Bristol.

I am one of the organisers of the web-seminar Spectral Geometry in the Clouds, together with Alexandre Girouard and Jean Lagacé.
