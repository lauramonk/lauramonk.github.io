Webpage of Laura Monk

I am a research associate at the University of Bristol (UK), where I work with Jens Marklof under the grant EP/W007010/1.
Before that, I did my PhD under the supervision of Nalini Anantharaman at the Université de Strasbourg, in France. I also stayed at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn (Germany), in the team of Ursula Hamenstädt.

I am one of the organisers of the web-seminar Spectral geometry in the clouds, together with Alexandre Girouard and Jean Lagacé.
